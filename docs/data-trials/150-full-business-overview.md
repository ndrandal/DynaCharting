## Data Sources

- Departments: `department_revenue()` — 8 depts as instancedRect@1
- Products: `product_rankings(10)` — 10 products as horizontal instancedRect@1
- Customers: `customer_tier_breakdown()` — 3 tiers as donut (triSolid@1)
- Workforce: `employee_hours(10)` — 10 employees as instancedRect@1
- Profit: `monthly_profit()` — 22 months as lineAA@1
